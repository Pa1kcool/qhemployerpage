We are also building robust AI Recruitment platform.

Why recruiters need to opt for a Premium Recruiter plan?

Premium Recruiter plan is enhanced with Real time Employer Dashboard updates, Premium Job post and unique Video Job alert feature.

How QUANTUMHUNTS can make hiring easier?

Client Job requirement will be posted in our QUANTUMHUNTS Job Platform as premium job and alert will be sent to all relevant candidates in our platform. This will be done on the same day of receiving the requirement. Unique Job Link will be created and posted in our official social media accounts. Video job alert will be created by the QUANTUMHUNTS video creation team and will be reshared in all social media QUANTUMHUNTS official page. This will be done within 48 hours of receiving the requirement.

What kind of candidates, a recruiter can find in QUANTUMHUNTS?

QUANTUMHUNTS Job platform has candidates from diverse backgrounds, Industry domains and experience.

How to post a job in QUANTUMHUNTS?

  1. Goto QUANTUMHUNTS Employer Page
  2. Create a Employer login
  3. Click on Post a Job for free
  4. Update all details, preview the Job and post it for review.
  5. The Job will go live within 1 hour of Job posting.

Why should HR consultant post a job with relevent Employer Details?

Candidates always apply for a job, only if they have all the relevant data from the employer. Providing employer detail will also attract more applicants.

Can recruiter manage all the posted jobs in QUANTUMHUNTS?

Yes. Recruiters can manage all the posted jobs. Any changes in QUANTUMHUNTS Job post will be reviewed, verified and updated in the Job portal by QUANTUMHUNTS Technical team.

Can recruiters use free plan within QUANTUMHUNTS Job platform?

Employer can login and post jobs for free for a 30 day trial period. The best part is " Recruiters don't have to provide payment details". Recruiters and Employers can enjoy stress free 30 day trial period.
